Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

1. Who We Are

Traigent Ltd. ("Traigent", "we", "us") is the data controller. Address: Hartglas 16, Tel-Aviv, Israel. Contact: privacy@traigent.ai.

2. What We Collect

  • Account data: name, email, company, role.
  • Billing data: processed by Paddle; we do not store full card details.
  • Service data: AI agent configurations, optimization inputs, outputs, and run metadata you provide.
  • Support data: messages you send us.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, access times, and service usage for security and billing.

No sensitive data: We do not collect or process special category data (health, race, religion, political opinions, biometric data, etc.).

No third-party analytics or advertising cookies: we do not use Google Analytics, Mixpanel, or similar trackers.

2.1 Execution Modes

  • Cloud Service: your optimization data is processed on our servers.
  • Local SDK: when you run the open-source SDK locally, data stays on your infrastructure and is not sent to Traigent unless you choose to connect to our cloud Service.

2.2 Children

The Service is not intended for children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect their data.

3. How We Use Data

  • Provide, secure, and improve the Service.
  • Process payments and send invoices/receipts.
  • Respond to support requests and send service communications.
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

Model training: We do not use your optimization data, configurations, outputs, or logs to train Traigent models or third-party models.

4. Legal Bases (EEA/UK/GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area, UK, and other jurisdictions with similar data protection laws, we process personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Performance of contract: providing the Service and billing.
  • Legitimate interest: security, fraud prevention, service improvement.
  • Consent: marketing communications (only if you opt in).
  • Legal obligation: accounting, tax, and regulatory compliance.

We follow GDPR principles in our data handling practices: we collect only necessary data, retain it only as long as needed, secure it appropriately, and respect your data subject rights (see ยง8).

5. Sharing and Processors

  • Paddle.com Market Limited: billing, tax, invoicing, refunds (UK/EU).
  • Cloud infrastructure provider(s): hosting and storage (US/EU regions).
  • Email delivery provider: transactional email (US).
  • Legal/authorities: when required by law or to protect rights.

We do not sell personal data. A current list of sub-processors is available upon request at privacy@traigent.ai.

6. International Transfers

Data may be processed in Israel, the EU, the UK, and the US. Where required, we use Standard Contractual Clauses or other approved safeguards. Israel is recognized by the EU as providing adequate protection.

7. Retention

  • Account and optimization data: for the life of your account and 30 days after cancellation, then deleted on request.
  • Payment and invoice records: up to 7 years (legal/tax obligations).
  • Support communications: typically 2 years or as required by law.
  • Server/security logs: typically 30 days.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, export, or object to processing, and to withdraw consent for consent-based processing.

To exercise these rights, email privacy@traigent.ai. We may request proof of identity. You may also lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

9. Cookies

We use only essential cookies for session management and security (e.g., session and CSRF tokens). We do not use analytics, advertising, or cross-site tracking cookies.

10. Security

  • Encryption in transit and at rest.
  • Access controls and authentication.
  • Regular security reviews.

Report security issues to security@traigent.ai.

11. Changes

We may update this Policy. Material changes will be notified via email or in-product before they take effect. The "Last updated" date will be revised.

12. Contact

Questions or requests: privacy@traigent.ai.
