Refund Policy

Last updated: December 2025

1. Overview

This Refund Policy explains when and how refunds are issued for Traigent Ltd.'s cloud Service. All payments and refunds are processed by Paddle.com Market Limited ("Paddle"), our Merchant of Record.

2. Free Trial

We offer a 7-day free trial. If you cancel before the trial ends, you will not be charged. If a payment method is on file and you do not cancel, your chosen plan will start automatically at the end of the trial.

3. Subscription Refunds

  • Annual plans: Prorated refunds are available within 30 days of purchase or renewal.
  • Monthly plans: No refunds for partial months; access continues until the end of the current billing period.
  • Billing errors/duplicates: Refunded in full when reported within 30 days.

4. Usage and Overage Charges

Usage beyond your plan's included allocation is billed per optimization trial as shown on the pricing page and at checkout. Usage/overage charges already incurred are not refundable.

5. Non-Refundable Items

  • Usage/overage charges already consumed
  • Monthly subscription fees after the billing period has begun
  • Annual fees after the 30-day refund window
  • Third-party AI provider costs you incur directly with those providers

6. How to Request a Refund

  • Email billing@traigent.ai with your account email and reason for the request.
  • We typically respond within 3 business days.

7. Processing

  • Approved refunds are processed by Paddle to the original payment method.

  • Refunds are issued in the original currency of payment and include applicable taxes collected on the original charge.

8. Cancellation and Data

  • Cancel anytime through your account or by contacting us; access remains until the current period ends.
  • We retain your optimization data for 30 days after expiration, then delete it upon request, except for records we must keep (e.g., billing records for up to 7 years; support communications as legally required).

9. Consumer Rights

Nothing in this policy affects any statutory rights you may have under applicable consumer protection laws.
