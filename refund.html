1. Overview

At Traigent, we want you to be satisfied with our AI agent optimization platform. This Refund Policy explains when and how refunds are provided. All payments and refunds are processed by Paddle.com Market Limited ("Paddle"), our Merchant of Record.

2. Free Trial

We offer a 7-day free trial for new users. No payment information is required during the trial. If you choose not to continue after the trial, you will not be charged. We encourage you to fully evaluate the Service during this period.

3. Refund Eligibility

Subscription TypeRefund Policy
Annual subscription Eligible for a prorated refund if you cancel within 30 days of purchase or renewal. After 30 days, no refund is available, but you retain access until the subscription period ends.
Monthly subscription No refunds for partial months. When you cancel, your access continues until the end of the current billing period. You will not be charged for subsequent months.
Billing errors Full refund for duplicate charges, incorrect amounts, or other billing errors. Contact us within 30 days of the charge.

3.1 Additional Refund Circumstances

You may also be eligible for a refund if:

  • The Service was materially unavailable for an extended period due to our fault
  • You experienced a significant technical issue that we could not resolve within a reasonable time
  • We made material changes to the Service that significantly reduce its value to you

4. Non-Refundable Items

The following are generally not eligible for refunds:

  • Monthly subscriptions after services have been rendered for that period
  • Annual subscriptions after the 30-day refund window
  • Usage-based charges already consumed
  • One-time setup or onboarding fees (if applicable)

5. How to Request a Refund

  1. Email us at billing@traigent.ai
  2. Include your account email address
  3. Describe the reason for your refund request
  4. We will respond within 3 business days

6. Refund Processing

  • Approved refunds are processed by Paddle to your original payment method
  • Refunds typically appear within 5-10 business days, depending on your bank or payment provider
  • You will receive confirmation from Paddle when the refund is processed
  • Refunds are issued in the original currency of payment

7. Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time:

  • Through your account settings in the Traigent dashboard
  • Via the Paddle customer portal (link in your invoice emails)
  • By contacting us at billing@traigent.ai

When you cancel:

  • Your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period
  • You will not be charged for the next billing period
  • You can continue using paid features until your subscription expires
  • Your data will be retained for 30 days after expiration, then deleted

8. Auto-Renewal

Subscriptions automatically renew at the end of each billing period unless cancelled. You will receive a reminder email before renewal. You can disable auto-renewal at any time through your account settings or the Paddle customer portal.

9. Your Statutory Rights

Important: This Refund Policy does not affect your statutory rights under applicable consumer protection laws. If you are a consumer in the European Union, United Kingdom, or other jurisdictions with mandatory consumer protection laws, you may have additional rights that cannot be waived by contract.

For EU/UK consumers: You may have the right to cancel a subscription within 14 days of purchase under the Consumer Rights Directive (cooling-off period), subject to certain exceptions for digital services. If you begin using the Service during this period with your explicit consent, you acknowledge that you may lose this right of withdrawal.

10. Disputes

If you are dissatisfied with our refund decision:

  1. Contact us at billing@traigent.ai to discuss your concerns
  2. If unresolved, you may contact Paddle's customer support
  3. You retain any rights available under applicable consumer protection laws
