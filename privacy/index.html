Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026 (rev. 2)

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy explains how Traigent Ltd ("Traigent", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects personal information when you visit https://traigent.ai, use https://portal.traigent.ai, request a demo, create an account, or use our products and services.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account details such as name, email address, and organization name.
  • Billing and transaction data needed to process subscriptions and invoices.
  • Product usage, diagnostic, and security information such as IP address, browser details, pages visited, and audit logs.
  • Communications you send to us, including support, sales, and onboarding requests.

3. How We Use Information

  • Provide, secure, support, and improve the Traigent platform.
  • Authenticate users, manage accounts, and prevent abuse or fraud.
  • Process subscriptions, payments, renewals, and customer support requests.
  • Communicate operational notices, product updates, and service changes.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms and policies.

4. Sharing and Processors

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers that help us operate the service, such as hosting, analytics, authentication, communications, and payment providers. Paddle acts as our Merchant of Record for subscription payments and may process billing information in accordance with its own policies and applicable law.

5. Subprocessors and Service Providers

We use third-party service providers to help us operate the Service (for example, hosting, security, communications, and billing). Depending on the feature, these providers process personal information on our behalf.

Core service providers (used to run the Service):

  • Paddle (Paddle.com Market Ltd) for Merchant of Record and payment processing.
  • Amazon Web Services, Inc. for cloud infrastructure hosting of the Traigent portal and API services.
  • Cloudflare, Inc. for CDN, DNS, and security protections.
  • Google LLC / Google Workspace for email and customer communications.
  • GitHub, Inc. / Microsoft for source code hosting and static hosting of the marketing website.

Optional AI model providers (used only if you select or enable them in your workspace):

  • OpenAI, LLC when OpenAI models are selected for a workflow.
  • Anthropic, PBC when Anthropic models are selected for a workflow.

If you do not select a given AI model provider for a workflow, we do not send Customer Content to that provider.

6. Retention

We retain personal information for as long as needed to provide the service, maintain security records, comply with contractual and legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. We delete or anonymize information when it is no longer required.

7. Cookies, Analytics, and Operational Telemetry

Cookies and similar technologies. We use strictly necessary cookies and similar technologies to provide the Service (for example, to keep you signed in, maintain session state, and protect the Service). We do not set cookies for cross-site advertising. We do not use analytics cookies on our marketing website. If we introduce non-essential cookies or similar technologies in the future, we will provide a consent mechanism where required.

Website and portal analytics. We use Cloudflare for CDN, DNS, and security protections. We do not use third-party advertising trackers. We do not use Cloudflare Web Analytics unless we explicitly deploy it on a site property. When Cloudflare Web Analytics is enabled, it is configured to operate without client-side cookies or localStorage.

Operational telemetry (server-side). We collect and use operational telemetry to operate, secure, and debug the Service. This includes server logs, security events, and performance data such as request metadata and traces. We store and analyze this telemetry within our cloud environment, and it is processed by the service providers listed in Section 5 to the extent they provide hosting, security, and monitoring capabilities for the Service.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to certain processing of your personal information. To make a privacy request, contact privacy@traigent.ai.

9. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal information. No internet or storage system is perfectly secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. International Data Transfers

Traigent Ltd is based in Israel. If you are located in the EEA, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, your personal information will be transferred to Israel for processing. Israel benefits from a European Commission adequacy decision for transfers from the EEA.

We also use service providers that process personal information in the United States and other countries. Where required for transfers from the EEA, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland to a country without an adequacy decision, we use appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses, and where applicable we rely on the EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework for recipients that are certified under it. You can request information about these safeguards by emailing privacy@traigent.ai.

11. California Privacy Notice (CCPA/CPRA)

This section applies to California residents and supplements the rest of this Privacy Policy.

Notice at Collection (categories and purposes). In the last 12 months, we collect the following categories of personal information:

  • Identifiers (e.g., name, email address, account identifiers, IP address).
  • Commercial information (e.g., subscription status and billing records).
  • Internet or other electronic network activity information (e.g., usage and diagnostic data for the Service).
  • Professional or employment-related information (e.g., organization name and role when provided).
  • Communications (e.g., support, sales, and onboarding messages you send us).

We collect this information from you, from your use of the Service, and from our service providers. We use it to provide and secure the Service, manage accounts, process subscriptions, provide support, communicate with you, and comply with law.

Sale and sharing. We do not sell personal information. We do not share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

Disclosures for business purposes. We disclose the categories above to service providers for business purposes such as payments (Paddle), cloud hosting (AWS), security (Cloudflare), and communications (Google Workspace), as described in Section 5.

Sensitive personal information. We do not use or disclose sensitive personal information for purposes other than those permitted by the CCPA/CPRA.

Your California privacy rights. Subject to applicable exceptions, you have the right to request: (1) access or know, (2) deletion, and (3) correction of your personal information, and the right not to receive discriminatory treatment for exercising these rights.

How to exercise your rights. Submit a request by emailing privacy@traigent.ai. We will verify your identity before completing certain requests. You may designate an authorized agent to make a request on your behalf.

12. Changes and Contact

We may update this policy from time to time by posting a revised version on this page. Questions about this policy can be sent to privacy@traigent.ai.
