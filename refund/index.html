Refund Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026 (rev. 2)

1. Overview

This Refund Policy applies to paid subscriptions purchased for Traigent. Paddle acts as Merchant of Record for transactions processed through our checkout flow.

2. Refund Window

Eligible paid plan charges include a 7-day money-back guarantee. This refund window applies to initial purchases, renewals, and upgrades. To request a refund, contact us within 7 days of the applicable charge date with the email address used for purchase and your transaction details.

3. What Is Generally Not Refundable

  • Charges requested after the stated refund window has expired.
  • Consumed usage-based services or professional services already delivered.
  • Accounts suspended or terminated for misuse or violation of our terms.
  • Amounts that are otherwise non-refundable under applicable checkout terms, except where refund rights are required by law.

4. How to Request a Refund

  1. Email billing@traigent.ai from the account used to purchase the subscription.
  2. Include your invoice, receipt, or Paddle transaction reference if available.
  3. Describe the reason for the request so we can review it promptly.

5. Cancellations

You may cancel a subscription at any time. Cancellation stops future renewals, but does not automatically create a refund for charges that have already been processed. You will retain access to paid features until the end of the current billing period unless we state otherwise in writing.

6. Processing

Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method through Paddle. Processing times depend on the payment method and financial institution.

7. Contact

Questions about refunds or billing can be sent to billing@traigent.ai.
